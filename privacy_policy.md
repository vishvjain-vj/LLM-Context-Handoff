# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

LLM Context Handoff is a browser extension that helps users compress and copy the contents of their current AI chat session for migration between AI platforms.

## Information We Access

The extension accesses the content of the active AI chat page only when the user explicitly activates the extension.

## Data Collection

We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

Conversation content is processed solely for generating a compressed context summary and copying it to the user's clipboard.

## Data Storage

The extension does not permanently store conversation content on our servers.

## Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or transfer user data to third parties.
